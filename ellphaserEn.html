About the game

Ellphaser is a challenging platformer where a young elephant must restore peace to his frozen mountain after a magical accident.

Jump carefully, as each leap shifts obstacles between solid and intangible states! Spikes, treacherous pits, and electric orbs are just some of the dangers in this dynamic and unpredictable world.

For fans of platformers with unique mechanics, Ellphaser delivers an intriguing and captivating experience. With charming pixel art and challenges that test your wits and reflexes, you'll need to plan every move to overcome the obstacles.

Get ready for an exciting journey! Can you gather the fragments of the Guardian Crystal and save the mountain?

Game Features

- Puzzle-platforming
- Varied challenges
- Relaxing and captivating soundtrack
- Adorable pixel art style
