About the game

Kinduo is a local cooperative game where two elemental friends overcome obstacles to return to their island.

Push boxes, use electricity to connect objects, break ice blocks, blow things up, and more in this cute and colorful cooperative game!

For those who like puzzle platform games, this is a fun and relaxing experience, with lovely pixel-style art and addictive game mechanics. Each character has its own unique ability, making the game more challenging and fun. Can you solve these puzzles?

No matter if you are playing solo or with a partner, get ready to have fun!

Highlights

- Puzzle platform mechanics
- Single-player and two-player local modes
- Relaxing Aesthetics
- Cute pixel-style art
