About the game

Some greedy businessmen decided to destroy the forest, to create the construction of a giant building. And with this several animals were expelled from their homes, seeing this, your role will be to prevent this this construction continue, eliminating all robots in the area and thus succeeding in saving the forest.

In this atypical game, your role is to stop the robot workers from from finishing the construction to save the forest!

Game Features

- One player
- 50 stages with more than 8 mechanics!
- Different ways to win each stage!
- Different ways to eliminate enemies!
