About the game

Some pets got lost from their owners in an office and now they have to work together.
Use boxes, mats, climb on shelves, tables, cupboards-tables or just make a mess trying to find them in this colorful cooperative game!

Game Features

- Puzzle platform mechanics
- Single-player and two-player local modes
- Relaxing Aesthetics
- Cute pixel-style art
