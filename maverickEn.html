History

Maverick is a rebel who challenges the tyrant using a mysterious and powerful technology to enslave his country. On a journey full of dangers and surprises, he seeks freedom for his people and himself.

About the game

Maverick's Fury is a 'Side Scrolling' shooting platform game, where only 1 shot takes down the player. The game was developed during a week-long game jam, and I've always wanted to create a commercial version of it. The game was crafted in collaboration with the artist dott_Chaos.
