About the game

Welcome to Europe, home of one of the most cruel and deadly diseases to mankind, the plague, the Black Death.

Pestilent Hunter is a multi enemy shooter game based on the Black Death, with several playable characters and several bosses to face! Create crazy buildings with the various artifacts you acquire throughout the game and try not to be killed by the various enemies by the various enemies that appear with each new scenario.

Game Features

- 4 playable characters with different weapons!
- More than 30 artifacts with different capabilities!
- 4 amazing and difficult bosses!
- Lots of enemies to kill!
